# Stadium Knowledge Base - MetLife Stadium

## Sections & Seating
- **Section 101-110**: Lower bowl, west side. Closest to main entrance and accessible seating.
- **Section 111-120**: Lower bowl, south end. Near team tunnels and concessions.
- **Section 201-220**: Upper bowl, all sides. Great views, requires elevator access.
- **Section 301-310**: Club level. Premium seating with exclusive lounges.

## Gates
- **Gate A (Main)**: Main entrance, north side. Primary entry for general admission.
- **Gate B (East)**: East side entrance. Near Section 114-115.
- **Gate C (South)**: South end. Closest to parking lots P1-P3.
- **Gate D (West)**: West side. Near premium entrance and club level elevators.
- **Gate VIP**: Private entrance for club/suite holders.

## Accessible Facilities
- **Accessible Restroom - Section 105**: Located at Row 15, Level 1. Gender-neutral option available.
- **Accessible Restroom - Section 114**: Near Gate B. Large stall, grab bars, sink at accessible height.
- **Accessible Restroom - Section 210**: Upper bowl, near Section 209. Elevator-accessible.
- **Accessible Restroom - Club Level**: Inside Club Lounge, accessible via elevator from Gate D.
- **Quiet Room (Sensory)**: Located behind Section 108. Low lighting, noise-cancelled, comfortable seating.
- **Service Animal Relief Area**: East side, near Gate B, adjacent to accessible restrooms.

## Elevators & Ramps
- **Elevator 1 (Main)**: Gate A → Section 201-210. Operational.
- **Elevator 2 (East)**: Gate B → Section 111-120. Operational.
- **Elevator 3 (Club)**: Ground → Club Level (Section 301-310). Operational.
- **Ramp A**: Main entrance to lower bowl. 6% grade, handrails both sides.
- **Ramp B**: Gate C to Section 111-120. 8% grade.
- **Ramp C**: East side to upper bowl. 10% grade, rest platforms every 20m.

## Concessions - Dietary Options
- **Halal Kitchen**: Section 112, Gate B side. Chicken/Beef halal bowls, kebabs.
- **Kosher Stand**: Section 108. Kosher hot dogs, nachos, pretzels.
- **Allergen-Free Zone**: Section 115. Gluten-free, nut-free options clearly labeled.
- **Vegetarian/Vegan**: All concession stands. Look for green "V" icons.
- **Premium Bar**: Club Level. Full bar, craft beers, wine selection.

## First Aid & Medical
- **First Aid Station 1**: Section 105, near Row 10. Open during all events.
- **First Aid Station 2**: Upper bowl, Section 210. First aid for upper level.
- **Medical Cart**: On standby at Gate C. Can be dispatched to any section.
- **AED Locations**: At all first aid stations, Gate entrances, and club lounges.

## Transit & Parking
- **Parking Lots**: P1 (main), P2 (east), P3 (south). Accessible parking in each lot.
- **Accessible Parking**: 150 spots in P1, 50 in P2, 30 in P3. Pre-reserved passes required.
- **Train Station**: MetLife Stadium station, 0.3 miles from Gate A. elevators available.
- **Bus Stop**: NJ Transit bus 160 stops at Gate C.
- **Drop-off Zone**: Main drop-off at Gate A. Wheelchair-accessible vehicles only.

## Policies
- **Service Animals**: Permitted. Must have valid documentation. Relief area behind Section 108.
- **Mobility Devices**: Wheelchairs and scooters permitted. Cannot block aisles.
- **Companion Seating**: One free companion ticket with accessible purchase.
- **Assistive Listening**: Headsets available at Guest Services (Gate A). Free deposit.
- **Sign Language Interpreters**: Request 48 hours in advance via accessibility@metlifestadium.com.
- **Early Entry**: Accessible entrances open 30 minutes before general admission.

## Emergency Information
- **Emergency Exit Routes**: Posted on back of all seats. Staff will guide during emergencies.
- **Assembly Point**: Parking Lot P1 for east sections, P3 for south sections.
- **Evacuation Alarms**: Red strobe lights + PA announcement. Staff will assist.
- **Tornado Shelter**: Interior stairwells on lower levels. Do not use elevators.
